Understanding Gold Purity

  • 24 KT Gold : 100% Pure Gold
  • 22 KT Gold : 91.6% Pure Gold
  • 18 KT Gold : 75% Pure Gold
  • 14 KT Gold : 58.3% Pure Gold

To Calculate the gold percentage are as follows

For example:

  • 24KT gold contains (24/24)*100=100% pure gold.
  • 22KT gold contains (22/24)*100=91.6% pure gold.
  • 18KT gold contains (18/24)*100=75% pure gold.
  • 14KT gold contains (14/24)*100=58.3% pure gold.

Basic Gold price Calculation Formula:

The fundamental formula for Calculation gold jewelry price is:

Formula: (Gold Rate per gram * Weight in grams) + Making Charges + GST + Hallmarking Charges = Final Price


Detailed Calculation Example

Let's Calculate the price of a 22k Gold Bangle Weighting 15.76gm


=> (7000*15.76) + 16548 + 3806 + 45
=> 1,30,719

Gold rate 22k 70,000 per 10gm 7,000 per gm
