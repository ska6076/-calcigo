Materials & Finishes

We work with premium materials to deliver exceptional quality and aesthetics.

Tinted Glass

Available in bronze, gray, green, and blue tints for glare reduction and privacy while maintaining natural light.

Frosted Glass

Acid-etched or sandblasted finishes for privacy without sacrificing light transmission. Perfect for bathrooms and partitions.

Low-E Glass

Low-emissivity coatings that reflect infrared light, keeping interiors cooler and reducing energy costs.

Tempered Glass

Heat-treated safety glass that's 4-5x stronger than regular glass. Essential for high-impact areas.

Laminated Glass

Two panes bonded with PVB interlayer for safety, security, and sound reduction.

Reflective Glass

Metal oxide coating for a mirror-like appearance that provides excellent solar heat control.

Frame Materials

Durable, stylish frames to complement any architectural style.

Aluminum Frames

Lightweight, strong, and corrosion-resistant. Ideal for modern designs with slim profiles and large glass areas.

Wood Frames

Classic warmth and natural beauty. Perfect for traditional and heritage properties with excellent insulation.

Vinyl Frames

Low maintenance and energy-efficient. Great for residential applications with excellent thermal properties.

Fiberglass Frames

Exceptional durability and strength. Can be painted any color and withstand extreme weather conditions.
