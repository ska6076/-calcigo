Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What types of glass do you offer?

We offer a wide range of glass types including tempered, laminated, low-E, tinted, frosted, reflective, and decorative glass. Each type serves different purposes for safety, energy efficiency, and aesthetics.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide complete installation services handled by our team of certified technicians. We ensure proper fitting, sealing, and finishing for long-lasting performance.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our products and installation work. Specific warranty periods vary by product type, typically ranging from 5 to 15 years.

Can I get a custom design?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom glass fabrication to meet your exact specifications. Our design team will work with you to create unique solutions for your project.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Standard window replacements take 1-2 weeks, while custom projects may take 4-8 weeks from measurement to installation.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations at our showroom or virtual meetings. Our experts will assess your needs and provide personalized recommendations.
